WHO WE REALLY ARE

GlitchCut Games™ is more than just a studio - we're a syndicate of dreamers, code slingers, and chaose architects crafting immersive worlds and unforgettable experiences. Born from a spark of rebellion, and the fact that great games do not stem from the African community, we create stories that punch through the ordinary. Starting as a different name, we fought through legal battles to register our previous name till we could no longer hold on and was changed to GlitchCut Games.
